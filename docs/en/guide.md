# Guide

## Mentor Passives

1. **Chi Dash** — Activation: C+C. Cooldown: 3s. A fast forward dash whose hitbox breaks normal and enhanced blocks.
   - Requires 6400 Chi and 6400 Nin

2. **Instant Rush / Faster STM Regen** — Your rush (W+W) is now instant and allows skill usage inside it, but still does not break enhanced block. Stamina regenerates significantly faster. Heal skip is also instant.
   - Requires 7100 Tai

3. **Stronger Block** — Most block-breakers do not break your block. Others take much longer to break it. Block regenerates faster. Cost: 100+ STM / 0.6s.
   - Requires 7000 HP

4. **Wider Dash** — Your dash becomes longer. Stacks with modes that use instant dash (e.g. Shock Cloak). Cost: 600 STM / dash.
   - Requires 6200 Tai

5. **Faster / Passive Charging** — Chakra regenerates 1.5–2x faster with passive regen of 2–3k per second.
   - Requires 6500 Nin

### Jiso Seishin (R1/C3)

- Best for defensive playstyles.
- Strong Block, fast block regen, high HP and fast chakra make you the best tank in the game.
- Easiest mentor to level up.
- Excellent in large-scale PvP: raids, wars, etc.
- Increased HP means heals restore more than usual.

**Works well with races:** Celestial, Puppet

### ✮ Shindai Akuma (R2/C2)

- The most mobile mentor in the game. Chi Dash + Wider Dash make you extremely fast.
- Combine a TP move with Chi Dash to instantly break an opponent's enhanced block.
- Use chakra jumps (Block + Jump) instead of enhanced block.
- Balanced damage stats between Tai and Chi skills. Suits any playstyle.

**Works well with races:** Puppet, Celestial

### Narumaki (R3/C4)

- More chakra means more damage.
- **Only** suited for team combat: 2v2, wars, etc.

::: danger Do not level for solo play
Narumaki is useless in 1v1 — raids and wars only.
:::

**Works well with races:** Celestial

### Bruce Kenichi (R4/C1)

- Spam taijutsu freely thanks to very fast stamina regen. Replace enhanced block with sky-run.
- Instant rush breaks an opponent's **normal** block at close range.
- After grabbing with rush, you can immediately use any skill — very powerful.
- Heal skip works instantly thanks to rush.
- Designed for 1v1 only.

**Works well with races:** Celestial, Puppet, Snake

## Races

A race can only be rolled when there is no red DNA on the icon. You cannot re-roll — you must obtain the DNA again first.

### Celestial (Kagoku Event Boss)

- +2000 max HP (total 12000).
- Double Shift — fly in any direction for 2s at Shock Cloak run speed. Cooldown: 4s.
- Block absorbs more damage.
- Flight lets you extend long-range combos (replaces Chi Dash).

### Puppet (Ailee Minakami, Dunes)

- Double jump in any direction. Without a mode, best paired with Wide Dash.
- All actions available during jumps: skills, chakra heal, block for movement.
- Equip a mode with instant dash → Wide Dash forward → hold block. Covers huge distances very fast.
- Immune to burn effects from Fire, Inferno, etc.

### Snake (Kabu Cobra, Obelisk)

- Heals HP when receiving poison damage over time.
- E button: fires yellow energy snakes toward cursor and teleports. Cost: 30,000 Chi. Cooldown: 20s.
- Limited set-building options.

**Works well with mentors:** Shindai, Narumaki, Bruce

::: warning Useless races
**Shinobi / Shark / Human** — not worth your time.
:::

## Terms

**Perfect Block (PB)** — after taking 200k+ damage you can block even while stunned. Block+Attack (RMB+LMB) breaks out of a combo with a short iFrame. Afterwards, M1 is unavailable for 4–5 seconds.

**End-lag** — a brief stun at the end of a skill animation. Almost every skill has end-lag.

**Block Break (BB)** — a move that breaks a block.

**iFrame** — a skill that gives full invincibility for a short time.

**Grab move** — locks you onto the opponent during animation and deals damage.

**Andodge (Antidodge)** — skills or mechanics that prevent the use of dodge.

**Ragequit (RQ)** — disconnecting during danger/PvP.

### Stun Types

- **Placelock Stun** — locks the model and hitbox to a fixed point. Most reliable. Examples: Bankai 2nd move, Rykan Blade.
- **AOE Stun** — a zone that continuously stuns anyone inside. Examples: Powder 2nd move, Sound Ken 4th move.
- **Ragdoll Stun** — disables player physics. Unreliable. Examples: Ember Entry, Acrobat Style, Raion-Rengoku 3rd move.
- **Knockback Stun** — stun from being pushed back. Examples: Dunes Fate Spirit 1st stage, Mecha-Spirit 1st move.

## Slot Bug

::: warning Read fully before attempting
To increase your slot count (e.g. 2→5, 3→7, 4→8):

1. Log in from your phone.
2. Create a set — equip the skills you want in advance and save it.
3. Equip the bloodline you want to bug.
4. Press Load on the set and **quickly** switch to the bloodline tab — tap the skill before it swaps out.

**Limitations:**
- Does not work with elements.
- If you place skills on the second bar (FGH) instead of the main one (VBN) — you must have a real bloodline equipped on FGH, otherwise you will get the error «Must have equipped Bloodline/Element».
:::
