# Tips & Tricks

## Combos & Mechanics

### Minakaze / Senko Storm — teleport

Place a kunai on the opponent, then use Pyromania skill 4 and click on them. This has a good chance of bypassing their block — the ragdoll from Pyromania will remove it completely.

### Minakaze + Senko Storm — marks

The marks from Minakaze and Senko Storm kunais are the same skill regardless of which you press. They do not put each other on cooldown.

### Escape from Dokei stun (1st)

During Dokei skills 1 and 3 you can press rush and clicks to escape the stun.

### Tai Renshiki — breaking dodge

After the skill, once you finish the air combo with a jump, the opponent cannot use dodge for another 2 seconds.

### Bruce — air combo bonus damage

Use Bruce's first skill, wait until near the end of the air combo, press jump — then immediately use the second skill.

### Menza Delay

The Menza sub-ability has an aura active for 2 more seconds after the skill ends.

### Air combo (+damage vs high ping)

3 clicks + jump (fast) during an air combo deals bonus damage to opponents with high ping.

### Rengoku + Jiso — Maru Daggers protection

When using the Rengoku mode with Jiso Seishin mentor, you have a high chance of not getting hit by Maru Daggers.

### Skip Heal

After using a heal, press rush to cancel the end-lag. Works best on the Bruce mentor.

### 2 Gens — infinite Z

Rush at an opponent or a training log, then press Z — you will stay in tailed form indefinitely without spending MD (shots still cost). Can be cancelled by a ragdoll, after which you are completely helpless.

## Tips

- Use Pumpkin Slayer instead of Maru Daggers.
- Pyromania 6th skill: restore chakra, stun from range, get behind the opponent and ragdoll.
- Order 1st: stun/block-break from long range.
- After two clicks in an air combo, start healing chakra and press jump.
- Use a heal inside an air combo.

## Useful Information

- More Nin = faster chakra regen.
- Damage is increased if the target has a burn effect.
- Kamaki Akuma 3rd skill can intercept any grab moves.
- Seishin 1st skill has the longest-range genza.
- Fan of Mustang explosion is a block-breaker.
- Bat Cursed Spirit (Rework) skills stun even if dodge was pressed in advance.
- Captain Jokei has an unbreakable block.
- Ink mode click kunais completely blind the enemy. 3rd skill too.
- Mentors can be leveled indefinitely.
- Lightning 6th — block-breaker (even Strong Block).
- Akuma bloodline has the longest dodge (5 seconds).
- Paper 3rd skill disables your joystick even during dodge.
- After completing a dungeon you receive ~35M XP for each equipped item — full stat level-up.
- Fastest Rell Coin farm: Open World → kage → wait 3 minutes → leave. Repeat.
- Skills like 10HV 2nd stage Z can be cancelled with a ragdoll skill (e.g. Z Forget Rengoku).
- Forget 1st skill while using 10HV 1st stage mode: one shot is replaced by auto-targeting orbs like 10HV 2nd stage.
